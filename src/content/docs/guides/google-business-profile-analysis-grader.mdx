### Quick Grading Formula (optional)

- **GBP Completeness (40%)** = completed checklist items / 20
- **Visibility (25%)** = map pack + organic presence
- **Citation Consistency (20%)** = clean listings ratio
- **Reputation Strength (15%)** = review volume, rating, and response rate

`Final Score = (0.40 × Completeness) + (0.25 × Visibility) + (0.20 × Citations) + (0.15 × Reputation)`
