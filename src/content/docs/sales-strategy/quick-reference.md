# Quick Reference Guide for Sales Operators

**Print this page and keep it with you for calls, meetings, and emails.**

---

## The Golden Rule

**We're not selling AI software. We're restoring teachers' time and giving administrators board-defensible proof.**

---

## Core Messaging Framework

### The Pain (Buyer Mirroring)
- Oklahoma is **50th nationally** despite **51% higher per-pupil spending**
- Teachers lose **65-75% of their time** to administrative work, not instruction
- This is a **time theft problem**, not a funding problem

### The Narrative (Teacher as Rockstar)
- **AI amplifies, never replaces** human teachers
- AI is a **co-pilot for routine tasks** (grading, differentiation, compliance)
- Frees teachers for **irreplaceable human work** (mentorship, relationships, inspiration)

### The Proof (Trial Kick-Off)
- **14-day Quick-Win Sprint** delivers board-ready metrics
- **Guaranteed deliverables:** Teacher time savings dashboard + achievement baseline
- **No-risk approach:** Measure first, decide later

---

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Target:** Oklahoma K-12 School Districts

**Decision Makers:**
1. **Superintendent** (Primary) - Cares about: Board defense, teacher retention, state ranking
2. **Curriculum Director** (Influencer) - Cares about: Oklahoma Academic Standards alignment, instructional quality
3. **Tech Director** (Gatekeeper) - Cares about: Implementation, data privacy, system integration
4. **School Board** (Final Approver) - Cares about: ROI, compliance, community perception

## Trial Kick-Off Meeting Structure (30 min)

### Part 1: Opening Hook (5 min)
**Script:**
> "You have a time theft problem. Your teachers spend 75% of their time on admin work, not instruction. Is that accurate?"

**Goal:** Get them to say "YES."

### Part 2: Solution Re-Frame (5 min)
**Script:**
> "AI amplifies, never replaces. We automate the 75% admin grind so teachers can focus on the irreplaceable human work."

**Visual:** Show AI Does/Doesn't chart

### Part 3: Trial Overview (10 min)
**Guaranteed Deliverables:**
1. Teacher time savings dashboard (30%+ time back)
2. Board-ready achievement baseline (engagement + standards alignment)

**Timeline:** 14 days from Kick-Off to Results Review

### Part 4: Objection Handling (5 min)
Use [Board Defense Q&A](/sales-strategy/board-defense)

### Part 5: Lock in Start Date (5 min)
**Script:**
> "What does your calendar look like for a Trial Kick-Off start date next week?"

**Confirm:** Pilot teachers, Tech Director point person, next board meeting date

## Top 5 Objections & Responses

### 1. "We don't have budget."
**Response:**
> "You're already spending on the problem—teacher turnover costs $20K-$30K per teacher. Retain 2-3 teachers and this pays for itself. The trial gives you data to justify the investment to your board."

### 2. "Teachers are overwhelmed."
**Response:**
> "That's why we designed this for busy teachers. Onboarding is 30 minutes. If they don't see immediate time savings in week 1, we adjust or pull back."

### 3. "What about data privacy?"
**Response:**
> "FERPA and COPPA compliant. Human-in-the-Loop protocol means AI never makes high-stakes decisions without teacher review. Full compliance docs for OSDE review."

### 4. "Will this replace teachers?"
**Response:**
> "No. Per OSDE guidance, AI augments, never replaces. AI handles grading and differentiation; teachers focus on mentorship and relationships. We make teachers the measurable heroes."

### 5. "We need board approval first."
**Response:**
> "Exactly. The trial exists to get you board-ready metrics. In 14 days, you'll have teacher time savings data and achievement baselines to make the case."

## Pre-Call Research Checklist

Before contacting a district, research:

- [ ] **District Size** (# of students, # of teachers)
- [ ] **Recent News** (board meeting notes, local news coverage)
- [ ] **State Ranking** (achievement data from OSDE)
- [ ] **Teacher Turnover** (if publicly available, or estimate from district size)
- [ ] **Tech Infrastructure** (do they have an LMS? Which one?)
- [ ] **Decision Makers** (LinkedIn profiles for Superintendent, Curriculum Director, Tech Director)

**Pro Tip:** Reference something specific from their recent board meeting in your first email (shows you did homework).

## Language to Avoid

**Don't Say:** → **Say Instead:**
- "AI-powered solution" → "AI co-pilot for teachers"
- "Cutting-edge technology" → "Level 4 compliant AI"
- "Increase efficiency" → "Restore teacher time"
- "Personalized learning platform" → "Differentiation that aligns with Oklahoma Academic Standards"
- "ROI" (to teachers) → "Get your time back"
- "Disruptive innovation" → "Amplifying what you already do well"

## Social Proof to Reference

**When they ask "Who else is using this?"**

**If you have Oklahoma districts:**
> "We currently work with [X] Oklahoma districts, including [Name 1-2]. I'm happy to connect you with their Curriculum Director for a peer reference."

**If you don't have Oklahoma districts yet:**
> "We're launching our first Oklahoma cohort this quarter. However, I can connect you with similar districts in [nearby state] who've seen [specific results]. That's why we're offering the trial—you get to see the data before committing."
