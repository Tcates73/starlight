# Thought Leadership Content Templates

## Overview

These templates are designed for **social media amplification, blog posts, and industry visibility**. The goal is to position VIBE STATE AI as thought leaders in the Oklahoma EdTech space while driving inbound interest from superintendents and curriculum directors.

**Publishing Cadence:**
- **LinkedIn:** 3x per week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday)
- **Blog/Medium:** 1x per week (long-form)
- **Twitter/X:** Daily (repurpose LinkedIn hooks)

**Core Messaging Themes:**
1. **Time Theft Problem** - Oklahoma teachers losing 65-75% of time to admin work

3. **Oklahoma-Specific Data** - 50th ranking, spending increases, compliance requirements

5. **Peer Success Stories** - Case studies from pilot districts
