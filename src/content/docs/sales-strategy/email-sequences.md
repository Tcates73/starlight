# 7-Touch Email Persuasion Loop

## Strategy Overview

This sequence is designed to move Oklahoma K-12 decision makers from **problem recognition** to **Trial Kick-Off Meeting commitment** through strategic psychological triggers.

**Timeline:** 14 business days (Touch every 2-3 days)

**Ultimate Goal:** Secure 30-minute Trial Kick-Off Meeting

---

## Sequence Management Tips

### Personalization Requirements
- **Always research the district** before sending Touch 1
- Include specific data: district size, current ranking, recent news
- Reference recent board meeting topics (check district website)
- Use actual teacher turnover numbers if available

### Response Handling
- **If they reply to Touch 1-2:** Skip to Trial Kick-Off booking (don't continue sequence)
- **If they ask for pricing:** Redirect to Trial Kick-Off (need metrics first)
- **If they raise objections:** Use Board Defense Q&A talking points
- **If they ghost after engagement:** Send Touch 6 early (don't wait for timeline)

### Multi-Threading Strategy
- **CC relevant stakeholders** starting at Touch 3
- **Loop in Technology Director** on Touch 2 (even if not primary contact)
- **Include Board members** on Touch 5+ (if appropriate)

### Success Metrics
- **Open Rate Target:** 40%+ (subject lines are optimized for urgency)
- **Response Rate Target:** 15-20% by Touch 3
- **Meeting Booking Rate:** 30% of respondents book Trial Kick-Off
