## Core Strategy Pillars

### 1. Buyer Mirroring Psychology
Reflect their pain points accurately before presenting solutions. We acknowledge:
- Oklahoma's 50th national ranking
- 51% higher per-pupil spending with no results
- 65-75% of teacher time consumed by administrative work
- Teacher burnout and retention crisis

### 2. Teacher as Rockstar Narrative
**AI amplifies, never replaces.** Our messaging positions AI as the "co-pilot" that:
- Automates administrative chaos
- Restores teacher time for human connection
- Makes teachers measurable heroes
- Enables Level 4 differentiated instruction

### 3. Regulatory Alignment
All messaging must reference Oklahoma-specific compliance:
- **Level 4 Oversight** requirements
- **Oklahoma Academic Standards** alignment
- **OSDE AI Guidance Version 2.0** compliance
- FERPA and COPPA data protection

### 4. Immediate Call-to-Action
Every touch point drives toward the **Trial Kick-Off Meeting** where we:
- Validate their pain in the first 5 minutes
- Frame the 14-day Quick-Win Sprint
- Guarantee board-ready metrics
